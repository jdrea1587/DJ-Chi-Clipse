DJ Chi’Clipse has been creating artful mixes with an ever increasing scope of influence since the early days of burning cd’s. Explore a colorful musical palette including world bass, deep house, jazz, funk, hip-hop, reggae, rock, sound-scapes, and ambient tunes to create a space for deep relaxation, focus, groove, and fun. He offers a unique blend of cross-genre composition, sensitive to set and setting and soothing for the soul. When he isn't studying full time to become a computer programmer, he enjoys teaching yoga and participating in a myriad of other activities
